The csv datasets concerning Helsinki City Bicycle Journeys are owned by City Bike Finland. The Origin–Destination (OD) datasets consist of journeys made by city bikes in Helsinki and Espoo.

Source url for Helsinki City Bicycle Journey Origin-Destination datasets:

https://www.hsl.fi/en/hsl/open-data

The Bicycle Journey csv files used in the app are from here:

https://dev.hsl.fi/citybikes/od-trips-2021/2021-05.csv https://dev.hsl.fi/citybikes/od-trips-2021/2021-06.csv https://dev.hsl.fi/citybikes/od-trips-2021/2021-07.csv

The Helsinki Region Transport's city bicycle stations
(Helsingin Seudun Liikenteen (HSL) kaupunkipyöräasemat)
are located in the cities of Helsinki and Espoo.

Source url for Helsinki Region Transport's city bicycle stations:

https://www.avoindata.fi/data/en_GB/dataset/hsl-n-kaupunkipyoraasemat/resource/a23eef3a-cc40-4608-8aa2-c730d17e8902

The dataset which has those stations is open data and is from here:

https://opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/726277c507ef4914b0aec3cbcfcbfafc_0.csv
